# Experience

## Open to All Forms of Learning

Vrindavan is open to both **day visits** and **overnight workshops**. Any activity that has a positive, enriching effect on people or society is welcome.

!!! info "Our Philosophy"
    The possibilities are open-ended — as long as the activity promotes focus, mindfulness, creativity, and social good.

## How It Works

### Open Workshop Model

Anyone — whether a Vrindavan partner or an outside expert — can conduct workshops or programs that align with Vrindavan's spirit and values.

**Who Can Conduct Workshops:**
- Vrindavan partners with diverse expertise
- External facilitators and subject matter experts
- Teachers, artists, craftspeople, and spiritual practitioners
- Community members with valuable knowledge to share

**Qualification Criteria:**
- Genuine expertise in the subject
- Commitment to positive learning environment
- Respect for Vrindavan's values and atmosphere
- Ability to engage participants meaningfully

### Financial Model

**Sustainable and Fair Approach:**
- Small participant fee covers facility maintenance
- Facilitators retain remaining amount as compensation
- Transparent structure with clear cost breakdown
- Accessible to participants while supporting sustainability

### Workshop Planning Process

**Initial Proposal:** Facilitators submit workshop description, objectives, duration, and resource needs
**Planning:** Collaborative discussion of details, logistics, and preparation
**Execution:** Vrindavan provides facilities, facilitator manages content and instruction
**Follow-up:** Evaluation, feedback, and future planning

## Seasonal Experiences

=== "Monsoon Season (June-September)"
    - Lush green landscapes and blooming flowers
    - Fresh, rain-washed air and cooler temperatures
    - Vibrant bird activity and natural sounds
    - Indoor activities during heavy rains

=== "Winter Season (October-February)"
    - Perfect weather for outdoor activities
    - Clear skies ideal for stargazing
    - Comfortable temperatures day and night
    - Peak season for workshops and events

=== "Summer Season (March-May)"
    - Early morning and evening activities
    - Shade from mature trees provides comfort
    - Fruit-bearing season for many trees
    - Natural cooling from vegetation
