# Activities & Learning

## Open to All Forms of Learning

Vrindavan is open to both **day visits** and **overnight workshops**. Any activity that has a positive, enriching effect on people or society is welcome.

!!! info "Our Philosophy"
    The possibilities are open-ended — as long as the activity promotes focus, mindfulness, creativity, and social good.

## Sample Workshop Formats

=== "Day Workshops"
    **Duration:** 6-8 hours
    
    **Schedule:**
    - Morning arrival and orientation
    - Pre-lunch session with hands-on activities
    - Community lunch and informal discussion
    - Afternoon intensive session
    - Reflection and sharing circle
    - Evening departure

=== "Weekend Retreats"
    **Duration:** 2-3 days
    
    **Schedule:**
    - Friday evening arrival and introduction
    - Saturday full-day intensive sessions
    - Sunday morning reflection and integration
    - Sunday afternoon departure
    - Optional overnight stay

=== "Extended Programs"
    **Duration:** 1 week or more
    
    **Features:**
    - Deep immersion in subject matter
    - Daily practice and skill development
    - Individual mentoring and guidance
    - Community living experience
    - Project completion and presentation
